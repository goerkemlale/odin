Greek Yoghurt Recipe

Nice and easy yoghurt for your palate

Ingredients

  • 1L Milk
  • Yoghurt yeast

    • How to?

    1. Put milk in a large bowl
    2. Medium heat for 4 hours
    3. Seperate the cream every 5 minutes
    4. When it hardens, cover with linen
    5. Refrigate 24 hours
    6. Done!
